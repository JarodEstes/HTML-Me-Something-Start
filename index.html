SMRC Plays In Frigid Tempatures

First Match

The first match that took place was nearly 0 degrees outside. The ground was completely frozen over along with ice caked on the top. Before the game we all huddled in a tent to keep warm. The game went about as you'd expect, but fortunately we did win this game.

Second Match

Second match is a bit warmer now. We lose this match in a tough game. Both teams are feeling the affects of being in minimal clothing while playing rugby.

Third Match

Our third match would be our final. We fall in this one to lose out of the tournament, but there was a silver lining in that. We lost one player to a dislocated shoulder, and another to a hurt ankle. We were beyond struggling to breath at this point.
